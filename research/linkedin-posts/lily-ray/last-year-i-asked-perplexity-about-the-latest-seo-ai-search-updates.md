Lily ray 5d edited report this post last year, i asked perplexity about the latest seo/ai search updates. It confidently told me about the september 2025 "perspectives" core update. The problem? That update never happened. It doesn't exist. It was fabricated by ai-generated content on agency blogs, cited by other ai-generated content, and eventually treated as fact by multiple llms. To this day, you can ask chatgpt, ai overviews, or ai mode about it and they'll explain how this fictitious update "fundamentally shifted how search results are ranked." 🙄 this is what i'm calling the ai slop loop: one hallucination gets scraped, republished, cited, and fed back into the same systems that created it. The cycle compounds, and the misinformation becomes the official narrative across ai platforms. I spoke with journalists at the bbc and the new york times about this problem and did some experiments to put it to the test. For one, i published a fake core update announcement on my blog and within 24 hours, ai overviews, ai mode, gemini, and chatgpt were serving it as fact. The results are scary, and the implications for the vast majority of ai users are not good. I wrote the full breakdown on my latest substack.
